ABOUT US

Trilogy​ ​is​ ​disrupting​ ​communication​ ​service​ ​delivery​ ​networks.​ ​We​ ​are innovators​ ​in​ ​a​ ​rapidly​ ​transforming​ ​wireless​ ​landscape.​ ​We​ ​help​ ​service providers​ ​navigate​ ​this​ ​transformation​ ​with​ ​our​ ​solutions.

We​ ​are​ ​a​ ​network-as-a-service​ ​company​ ​with​ ​cutting​ ​edge​ ​solutions​ ​for densification​ ​of​ ​wireless​ ​networks​ ​and​ ​virtualized​ ​network​ ​functions.​Our services​ ​are​ ​available​ ​as​ ​‘consumable’​ ​services.

Trilogy’s​ ​management​ ​team​ ​has​ ​over​ ​half​ ​a​ ​century​ ​of​ ​combined experience​ ​in​ ​the​ ​communication​ ​service​ ​industry.​ ​We​ ​are​ ​serial entrepreneurs​ ​with​ ​a​ ​passion​ ​to​ ​effect​ ​change​ ​and​ ​help​ ​our​ ​customer partners​ ​.
