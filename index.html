WHO WE ARE

Trilogy​ ​is​ ​disrupting​ ​communication​ ​service​ ​delivery​ ​networks.​ ​We​ ​are innovators​ ​in​ ​a​ ​rapidly​ ​transforming​ ​wireless​ ​landscape.​ ​We​ ​help​ ​service providers​ ​navigate​ ​this​ ​transformation​ ​with​ ​our​ ​solutions.

We​ ​are​ ​a​ ​network-as-a-service​ ​company​ ​with​ ​cutting​ ​edge​ ​solutions​ ​for densification​ ​of​ ​wireless​ ​networks​ ​and​ ​virtualized​ ​network​ ​functions.​ ​Our services​ ​are​ ​available​ ​as​ ​‘consumable’​ ​services.​ ​Our​ ​security​ ​solutions​ ​help protect​ ​our​ ​customer​ ​networks​ ​from​ ​security​ ​threats​ ​and​ ​vulnerabilities.

Trilogy’s​ ​management​ ​team​ ​has​ ​over​ ​half​ ​a​ ​century​ ​of​ ​combined experience​ ​in​ ​the​ ​communication​ ​service​ ​industry.​ ​We​ ​are​ ​serial entrepreneurs​ ​with​ ​a​ ​passion​ ​to​ ​effect​ ​change​ ​and​ ​help​ ​our​ ​customer partners​ ​to​ ​seize​ ​new​ ​opportunities​ ​with​ ​Internet​ ​of​ ​Things,​ ​next​ ​generation 5G​ ​networks​ ​and​ ​machine​ ​learning​ ​&​ ​artificial​ ​intelligence​ ​technologies.

About

CONEX

Conex​ ​is​ ​a​ ​service​ ​delivery​ ​platform​ ​that​ ​comes​ ​with​ ​core​ ​services​ ​required​ ​for​ ​telecom networks.​ ​It​ ​is​ ​a​ ​secure​ ​private​ ​cloud​ ​connecting​ ​network​ ​operators​ ​and​ ​solution​ ​providers.​ ​The services​ ​are​ ​virtualized​ ​and​ ​can​ ​be​ ​deployed​ ​in​ ​a​ ​very​ ​short​ ​period​ ​of​ ​time.​ Some of the Conex features are:

  • Connectivity
  • Provisioning
  • Monetization
  • Network Security
  • Customer Support
