THE PROBLEM

Communication networks are expensive and rigid

Service providers are usually locked into a single vendor and hardware-centric systems for a whole technology generation. Deployment and Integration of the elements are complex and takes months. As the administrative functions are distributed in each network element, managing the network is hard. Testing and launching new services introduces new integration and licensing issues. Such systems are not sustainable anymore as over the top solution providers and their new generation of cloud services threaten the service provider’s value proposition.

  • Flexible
  • Reliable
  • Secure
  • Scalable

Service providers need a platform that is flexible, reliable, secure and scalable on demand, so they can deliver the next generation of products for their customer

Virtualized network functions and software defined networking can help service providers get back into the game

OUR SOLUTION

Our unique approach to network virtualization takes each network function and makes them available as consumable services in a ‘plug and play’ model. Our flexible 'pay-as-you-use' model takes away barriers to test and launch new services.

What happened to computing is now happening to networks

Service providers can 'assemble' new solutions quickly by using our virtualized services. Our services includes core network functions, support systems and business services. Network engineers can experiment new ideas and ‘play’ in the sandbox with new services before integrating them into their live systems.

All of our services are available within a private network, completely secured and virtualized for each service provider. Network administrators have full view and control to the services that are enabled with our centralized management features.

Virtualization opens up new operating models and business models that can bring economies of scale
