Trilogy LTE to lead the rural wireless association’s (RTG) LTE roaming initiative

on 12.09.2017 at 9:30 PM (IST) by Trilogy Team

Trilogy LTE, a managed services provider, announced today they have been chosen to lead The Rural Wireless Association’s (RTG), LTE Roaming Initiative, a revolutionary program designed to facilitate LTE roaming on a rural, regional, national and international scale. Other members of The LTE Roaming Initiative include Performance Technologies, Inteliquent, Aicent, PSA and Planet Cellular.

The RTG’s LTE Roaming Initiative will provide the diameter networking equipment, professional services, peer-to-peer network facilities, IPX Interconnection, TAP record accounting and a selection of 4G LTE devices enabling the industry’s most comprehensive LTE roaming Eco-System.

“Wireless subscribers have long become accustomed to seamless automatic roaming service as they’ve traveled across their state, the nation and around the world,” stated Trilogy LTE Services, Inc. President and CEO, George Woodward. “We are thrilled to lead the RTG’s LTE Roaming Initiative, a critical first step in creating that same freedom and level of service on the new 4G LTE networks using diameter signaling, the new global roaming standard,” Woodward explained.

“RTG is excited to work with these strategic partners and help our rural carrier members leverage technology advancements to directly interconnect with one another, and eventually to the largest carriers in the world,” commented RTG Executive Director, Tanya Sullivan. “We are proud to support an initiative that will enable cost savings and provide a level of efficiency and quality the rural wireless industry has not been able to deliver before,” she stated.

To read RTG’s announcement, please visit: http://bit.ly/18ZM1eN

About RTG – Headquartered in Washington, DC, RTG – the Rural Wireless Association is a trade association representing rural wireless carriers who each serve fewer than 100,000 subscribers. RTG’s members have joined together to speed delivery of new, efficient and innovative telecommunications technologies to remote and underserved communities. For more information visit ruraltelecomgroup.org @RTGwireless

About Trilogy LTE Services, Inc. – Trilogy LTE is the pioneer of “Diameter as a Service” (DaaS) specifically focused on enabling cost effective roaming for America’s rural LTE operators. Trilogy’s managed service provides the technology and skills needed to enable the new Peer-to-Peer direct network connection option for rural operators. Trilogy installs and manages an in-network and cloud based Diameter Edge Agent (DEA) that provides a single secure, topology hiding, connection to the outside world. The company manages all diameter connections and performs end-to-end testing with full certification on every aspect of a carrier’s LTE roaming requirements. For more information, visit http://www.trilogy-lte.com
