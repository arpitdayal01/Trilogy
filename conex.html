INTRODUCING CONEX

Conex​ ​is​ ​the​ ​world’s​ ​first​ ​private​ ​cloud​ ​for​ ​the​ ​telecom​ ​industry.​

It​ ​interconnects​ ​network operators​ ​and​ ​solution​ ​providers​ ​in​ ​a​ ​secured​ ​private​ ​cloud​ ​to​ ​deliver​ ​new​ ​services,​ ​share resources​ ​and​ ​innovate​ ​on​ ​next​ ​generation​ ​technologies.

Conex​ ​as​ ​a​ ​service​ ​delivery​ ​platform​ ​comes​ ​with​ ​all​ ​the​ ​features​ ​required​ ​for​ ​telecom​ ​networks. All​ ​partner​ ​solutions​ ​come​ ​pre​ ​integrated​ ​to​ ​the​ ​Conex​ ​features,​ ​so​ ​Conex​ ​partners​ ​don’t​ ​have​ ​to replicate​ ​it​ ​for​ ​each​ ​of​ ​their​ ​solutions.

Conex​ ​is​ ​an​ ​unprecedented​ ​level​ ​of​ ​collaboration​ ​amongst​ ​network​ ​operators​ ​and​ ​solution providers.

You​ ​can​ ​monetize​ ​your​ ​network​ ​assets​ ​and​ ​resources​ ​when​ ​you​ ​make​ ​them​ ​available​ ​on Conex.​

Trilogy​ ​Partnership​ ​Program​ ​helps​ ​network​ ​operators​ ​deploy​ ​nationwide​ ​IoT​ ​networks and​ ​interconnect​ ​to​ ​global​ ​networks. Conex​ ​has​ ​a​ ​number​ ​of​ ​new​ ​technology​ ​solutions​ ​from​ ​Trilogy​ ​and​ ​its​ ​partners.​ ​The​ ​solutions are​ ​available​ ​as​ ​virtualized​ ​services​ ​with​ ​no​ ​expensive​ ​capital​ ​costs.
